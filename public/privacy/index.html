Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 2025

Data We Collect

When you use Pins, we collect the following information:

  • Google Account Information: Name, email address, and profile picture when you sign in with Google
  • Location Data: Geographic coordinates of points you mark on the map
  • Usage Data: Timestamps of when points are created

How We Use Your Data

  • To authenticate your account and provide personalized features
  • To store and display your marked locations on the map
  • To associate your points with your account for data persistence

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a trusted backend service. We implement security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access.

Your Rights

  • You can delete your account and all associated data at any time
  • You can sign out to stop data collection
  • You can contact us to request data deletion or access

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google OAuth: For authentication (subject to Google's Privacy Policy)
  • Supabase: For data storage (subject to Supabase's Privacy Policy)
  • OpenStreetMap/Nominatim: For location search

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or want to delete your data, please contact us through GitHub issues.
